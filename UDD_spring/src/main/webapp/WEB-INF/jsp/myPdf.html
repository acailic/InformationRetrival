About  
The novel The Remains of the Day was written by the english novelist Kazuo  
Ishiguro, who was born in Japan. The novel is known for its mixture of mod-  
ernistic and postmodernistic characteristics. Postmodernistic features can be  
seen in the unique narrative technique, the introduction of historical context,  
and the discussion about human dignity.
